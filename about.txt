Login System: Users (students and teachers) can log in with their credentials to access the system.

Student Attendance: Students can mark their attendance by logging in with their username and password.

Teacher Dashboard: Teachers can log in to view attendance records of all students.

Filtering Attendance: Teachers can filter attendance records to view only present students.

Database Integration: The system integrates with a MySQL database to store student information and attendance records.

GUI Interface: The project provides a graphical user interface (GUI) for easy interaction with the system.
